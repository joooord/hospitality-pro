Hidden Café Bar Social closed the week ending 15 March at £48.6k turnover across 612 covers, up 7.6% on prior week and £3.2k ahead of forecast. Food drove 58% of revenue and maintained a 65% GP after the spring small plates launch landed mid-week. Beverage delivered £17.2k with a 69% GP despite running the vinyl supper club pour programme.

Wage spend landed at £15.7k or 32.4% of revenue — inside tolerance after trimming late-shift support on Sunday. Food cost sat at 34.9% due to nduja and dairy inflation, partially offset by the hot-honey drizzle batch prep. Beverage cost tracked 31%, mainly due to sparkling allocations for the private listening group. All-in GP finished at 67% with £3k of other revenue from the day-part coffee subscription pilot.

Rail disruption on Saturday suppressed late-night trade by ~40 covers but was balanced by a strong Thursday influencer push led by Abi. Guest sentiment stayed positive with loyalty invites converting 18% of follow-ups.
